Rey Colino

Friday 8
DJ Set

Born and raised in the city of Brussels, Colin Volvert aka Rey Colino now resides in Amsterdam, although his heart remained in Brussels where he is a resident DJ of the infamous Under My Garage. He has quite a straightforward aim, to release, DJ and broadcast sparkling new sounds and unearth lost musical pearls through his Kalahari Oyster Cult label and his DJ sets.
